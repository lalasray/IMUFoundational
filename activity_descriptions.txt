Activity A: Walking
- Head: slight turn/rotate, occasional (pace: slow–medium)
- Torso: sway (continuous, pace: medium)
- Pelvis/Hips: shift forward/back, flex/extend (repetitive, pace: medium)
- Left Leg: hip flex/extend (repetitive, pace: medium), knee flex/extend (repetitive), ankle dorsiflex/plantarflex
- Right Leg: same as left
- Left Arm: arm swing forward/back (repetitive, pace: medium)
- Right Arm: same as left

Activity B: Jogging
- Head: nod or bob (frequent, pace: medium–fast)
- Torso: bounce (continuous, pace: medium–fast)
- Pelvis/Hips: pelvic tilt/shift (repetitive, pace: medium–fast)
- Left Leg: hip flex/extend (rapid, high pace), knee flex/extend, ankle dorsiflex/plantarflex
- Right Leg: same as left
- Left Arm: vigorous arm swing (rapid, pace: high)
- Right Arm: same

Activity C: Stairs
- Head: gaze shift up/down (occasional)
- Torso: lean forward (occasional)
- Pelvis/Hips: hip flex/extend (continuous), shift upward
- Left Leg: knee flex/extend (repetitive), hip flex/extend
- Right Leg: same as left
- Left Arm: may hold rail (static) or swing slightly
- Right Arm: same

Activity D: Sitting
- Head: hold still (static)
- Torso: slight lean (static)
- Pelvis/Hips: tilt posterior (static)
- Left Leg: knee flexed hold
- Right Leg: same
- Left Arm: rest on lap (static)
- Right Arm: same

Activity E: Standing
- Head: hold still (static) or slight nods
- Torso: upright hold, minor sway (occasional)
- Pelvis/Hips: neutral hold
- Left Leg: weight shift left/right (occasional)
- Right Leg: same
- Left Arm: hang at side (static)
- Right Arm: same

Activity F: Typing
- Head: look down (hold)
- Torso: leaning slightly forward
- Pelvis/Hips: neutral
- Left Arm: wrist flex/extend (fast, repetitive), forearm pronation/supination minimal
- Right Arm: same
- Fingers (contextual): finger spread/close rapid

Activity G: Brushing Teeth
- Head: turn/rotate side-to-side (repetitive)
- Torso: slight lean forward
- Pelvis/Hips: slight tilt forward
- Left Arm: elbow flex/extend, wrist circular (repetitive)
- Right Arm: may hold cup or steady

Activity H: Eating Soup
- Head: nod forward (occasional)
- Torso: slight lean forward
- Pelvis/Hips: neutral
- Left Arm: bring spoon to mouth (elbow flex/extend, wrist flex/extend)
- Right Arm: hold bowl (static)

Activity I: Eating Chips
- Head: tilt slightly, nod
- Torso: slight lean
- Pelvis/Hips: neutral
- Left Arm: bring chip to mouth (elbow flex/extend, wrist rotation)
- Right Arm: may be static or reach to bowl

Activity J: Eating Pasta
- Head: tilt forward
- Torso: lean forward
- Pelvis/Hips: neutral
- Left Arm: hold fork (static), wrist twist grip
- Right Arm: similar or holding plate

Activity K: Drinking from Cup
- Head: tilt-back/upward
- Torso: slight lean back
- Pelvis/Hips: neutral
- Left/Right Arm: bring cup to mouth (elbow flex/extend, wrist flex/extend), pace slow

Activity L: Eating Sandwich
- Head: nod slightly
- Torso: lean forward
- Pelvis/Hips: neutral
- Left/Right Arm: bring sandwich to mouth (elbow flex/extend)

Activity M: Kicking (Soccer Ball)
- Head: look towards ball
- Torso: lean forward/back depending on kick
- Pelvis/Hips: rotate and flex (prep)
- Left Leg: hip flex/extend preparing, knee flex/extend kicking
- Right Leg: planting leg static or shift
- Arms: out for balance (abduction, rotation)

Activity O: Playing Catch w/Tennis Ball
- Head: gaze shift tracking ball
- Torso: rotate to face thrower
- Pelvis/Hips: shift weight left/right
- Arms: shoulder flex/extend, elbow flex/extend catching/throwing

Activity P: Dribbling (Basketball)
- Head: look ahead (nods)
- Torso: lean forward slightly
- Pelvis/Hips: squat/shift weight
- Left/Right Arm: elbow flex/extend dribbling, wrist flex/extend with speed
- Legs: knee flex/extend bouncing movement

Activity Q: Writing
- Head: look down at paper
- Torso: slight lean
- Pelvis/Hips: seated neutral
- Left/Right Arm: wrist flex/extend writing, finger point/cock

Activity R: Clapping
- Head: hold still
- Torso: slight expansion
- Pelvis/Hips: neutral
- Arms: elbow flex/extend bringing hands together repeatedly (pace variable)

Pace ranges noted as slow/medium/high based on typical speed. Occurrence indicates repetitive vs occasional vs static.
