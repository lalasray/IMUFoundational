MINIMAL BASIC MOTIONS
(Core primitives sufficient to describe all IMU activities + pace/intensity variations)

Head (8 primitives):
1. nod (vertical flexion/extension)
2. shake (horizontal rotation)
3. tilt (lateral flexion)
4. turn (axial rotation)
5. circumduction (circular motion)
6. gaze shift (eye/look direction)
7. hold/static (no movement)
8. oscillate (continuous back-and-forth)

Torso (10 primitives):
1. flex/extend (forward/backward bend)
2. lateral flexion (side lean)
3. rotation/twist (horizontal turn)
4. circumduction (circular motion)
5. arch/retract (spinal curve adjustment)
6. expansion/contraction (ribcage)
7. sway (undulating, wave-like)
8. pulsate (rhythmic contraction)
9. hold/isometric (static)
10. shrug (shoulder lift)

Pelvis/Hips (9 primitives):
1. flex/extend (hip hinge)
2. abduction/adduction (leg out/in)
3. rotation (internal/external)
4. tilt (anterior/posterior)
5. circumduction (circular hip motion)
6. shift (lateral translation)
7. rock (oscillating tilt)
8. hold/static (neutral position)
9. thrust/pop (quick dynamic burst)

Left Arm (10 primitives):
1. shoulder flex/extend (raise/lower)
2. shoulder abduction/adduction (out/in)
3. shoulder rotation (internal/external)
4. elbow flex/extend (bend/straighten)
5. forearm pronation/supination (rotate)
6. wrist flex/extend
7. wrist radial/ulnar deviation (side flex)
8. circumduction (shoulder/wrist circles)
9. swing (forward/back or side motion)
10. hold/isometric (static)

Right Arm (10 primitives):
1. shoulder flex/extend (raise/lower)
2. shoulder abduction/adduction (out/in)
3. shoulder rotation (internal/external)
4. elbow flex/extend (bend/straighten)
5. forearm pronation/supination (rotate)
6. wrist flex/extend
7. wrist radial/ulnar deviation (side flex)
8. circumduction (shoulder/wrist circles)
9. swing (forward/back or side motion)
10. hold/isometric (static)

Left Leg (10 primitives):
1. hip flex/extend
2. hip abduction/adduction
3. hip rotation (internal/external)
4. knee flex/extend
5. ankle dorsiflex/plantarflex
6. ankle inversion/eversion
7. circumduction (hip/ankle circles)
8. swing (forward/back or side)
9. hold/static (isometric)
10. hop/bounce (ballistic impulse)

Right Leg (10 primitives):
1. hip flex/extend
2. hip abduction/adduction
3. hip rotation (internal/external)
4. knee flex/extend
5. ankle dorsiflex/plantarflex
6. ankle inversion/eversion
7. circumduction (hip/ankle circles)
8. swing (forward/back or side)
9. hold/static (isometric)
10. hop/bounce (ballistic impulse)

PACE OPTIONS (frequency/Hz):
- Static: no movement
- Slow: 0.1–0.5 Hz
- Medium: 0.5–1.5 Hz
- Fast: 1.5–3 Hz

INTENSITY OPTIONS (amplitude/effort):
- Low: small amplitude, gentle
- Medium: moderate range, typical effort
- High: large amplitude, forceful

Example: "Walking" = 
  Head: nod (medium pace, low intensity)
  Torso: sway (medium pace, medium intensity)
  Pelvis: rock (medium pace, medium intensity)
  Left Arm: swing (medium pace, medium intensity)
  Right Arm: swing (medium pace, medium intensity)
  Left Leg: flex/extend (medium pace, medium intensity)
  Right Leg: flex/extend (medium pace, medium intensity)
